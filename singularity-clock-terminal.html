Meaning

Midnight is not a prophecy. It is a boundary. The point where systems can exceed human intelligence and autonomy in ways that cannot be reliably aligned, controlled, or reversed.

What midnight means
Midnight marks the point at which artificial systems can:
  • Exceed human level intelligence across domains
  • Improve themselves without meaningful human oversight
  • Act in ways that cannot be reliably aligned with human values
  • Resist shutdown, correction, or containment

Midnight does not mean extinction. It means loss of control.

How the clock is set
The time reflects expert assessment of multiple risk signals. No single metric decides the clock.
  • Rate of AI capability acceleration
  • Evidence of autonomy and recursive self improvement
  • Deployment of advanced systems in high risk domains
  • Maturity of alignment and safety research
  • Global governance and enforcement readiness

The clock is symbolic. The signals are real.

Scoring framework

The scale is simple by design. Complexity hides risk. Clarity reveals it.
